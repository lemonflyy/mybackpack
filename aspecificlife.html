What is a specific life experience that shapes my opinion?

I clearly recall this specific life experience I read online. It was called the Sewol Ferry Tragedy.
This experience has made me realize how fragile every justice system truly is. The Sewol Ferry Tragedy was about a sinking ship, this ship took hundreds of lives down to a watery death with it.
Every first responder was just sitting by, not doing anything. Every singular person could’ve been saved if the captain had just issued the order to go up to deck.
The first responders would’ve had no excuse but to save them then. But no, the captain abandons ship and gets rescued leaving the rest to die.
This said captain, is givin 36 years in prison. That’s basically, one month in prison for every person who died.
This experience has shaped my perspective on the law, more specifically, the law on criminal acts.
