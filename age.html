How does my age affect my opinion?

I am young enough where I do not receive taxes. Unlike adults, I don’t have to worry about taxes. This brings me to my point where different civic issues will affect different age groups. Perhaps a civic issue is targeted towards senior citizens over toddlers.
For example, when people talk about lowering the houseing market, it’s targeted towards adults. Specifically, adults who are trying to buy houses.
Versus a topic such as the 2SLGTBQIA. This topic would be targeted more towards teens, as there’s more awareness on this topic in this age group. Civic issues are like a media’s target audience, they change by your age.
