How does language affect my opinion?

As I am able to comprehend multiple languages, I have a higher exposure to the various forms of media the government uses to sell their narrative.
Understanding different languages can help remember different intellectual properties by their famous quotation. Personally, I remember McDonalds by their slogan, I’m lovin’ it.

The Canadian education system is taught primarily in the Canadian national languages. The different languages lead to the exposure of different opinions on topics.
Such as, the Canadian Railroad. There was a difference in focus between the two languages. In French, we were taught primarily what the railroad was supposed to be used for, transportation of supplies in aid of a potential war.
However, in English speaking schools, the teachings of the railroad were focused on the atrocities commited on the minority groups, especially, the chinese immigrants.

In conclusion, language affects my opinion by helping me remember certain intellectual properties. It also changes what we’re taught and what perspective a person may have.
